Archaeology

Archaeology explores past human civilizations through material remains. This field helps us understand ancient cultures, technologies, and ways of life.

Importance of Archaeology

By studying artifacts and ancient sites, archaeologists uncover details about how societies evolved, interacted, and influenced one another.

Famous Archaeological Discoveries

Some of the most famous archaeological findings include the Rosetta Stone, the ruins of Pompeii, and the tomb of Tutankhamun.
